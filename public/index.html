Good to know

Questions parents ask

What ages can join?

Each camp lists its own age range on the calendar. Most summer sessions are for ages 5 to 12, but check the camp you pick.

What should my child bring?

Water, cleats, and shin guards, plus a ball if you have one. Any extra notes for a specific camp show up when you select it and on your confirmation.

How do payments work?

Payment is handled securely by Stripe Checkout. Your spot is confirmed once Stripe completes the payment, and you'll get a confirmation by email.

Can I sign up more than one child?

Yes. Use "Add another child" on the signup form to register siblings together in one checkout.

What about cancellations or weather?

If a session is rained out or you need to reschedule, reach out to Noah directly and we'll sort it out.
