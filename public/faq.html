Questions parents ask

A few quick answers before you sign your player up.

What ages can join?

Most camps are for ages 5 to 12, and every skill level is welcome — from first-timers to club players. Each camp shows its exact age range on the Camps page.

How big are the groups?

Small on purpose — capped at 5 players. That means more touches, more reps, and real attention for your child.

What should my child bring?

Water, cleats, shin guards, and a ball if you've got one. If a camp needs anything else, it'll say so when you pick it — and on your confirmation.

How do payments work?

You check out securely through Stripe. Your spot is locked in the moment the payment goes through, and a confirmation lands in your inbox.

Can I sign up more than one child?

Absolutely. Tap "Add another child" on the signup form to register siblings together in one checkout.

What if it rains or plans change?

It happens! If a session gets rained out or your schedule shifts, just reach out to Noah and we'll make it right.

Still have a question?

Reach out any time — contact details are at the bottom of the page.
